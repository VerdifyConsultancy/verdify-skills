## Cross-Project Gates

- Do not copy or print raw secrets in bootstrap packets, GitHub issues, dashboards, or North Star docs.
- Treat repo-to-namespace mapping and domain-agent authority as explicit data, not inference from names.
- Keep reusable lifecycle behavior in `verdify-skills`; keep runtime control-plane behavior in `agents`.
- Keep Gravity out of live control-plane orchestration unless a separate integration decision approves it.
- Keep VAST pricing and cloud-performance claims source-backed and date-stamped.
