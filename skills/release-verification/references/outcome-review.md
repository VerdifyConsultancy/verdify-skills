# Outcome review

Summarize from the user's perspective:

- intended sprint outcome;
- delivered workflows and visible changes;
- evidence and environment;
- deviations, known issues, and residual risks;
- deployment/rollback state;
- follow-up issues and owners;
- acceptance decision and rationale;
- lessons that should change definitions, contracts, policy, or tooling.

Do not overwhelm the human with raw logs; link evidence while explaining its meaning and limitations.
