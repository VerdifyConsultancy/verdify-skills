# Lane transaction

Lane decomposition and contract compilation are one transaction because topology is not safe until ownership and interfaces are concrete.

Before approval verify:

- no issue is assigned to two lanes;
- every lane has at least one issue;
- multi-issue lanes contain an accepted coupling justification;
- owned/prohibited paths do not conflict;
- hard dependencies form an executable order;
- runtime namespaces avoid collisions;
- every criterion has a validation/evidence path;
- branch names and baseline SHAs are fixed;
- material change triggers are explicit.
